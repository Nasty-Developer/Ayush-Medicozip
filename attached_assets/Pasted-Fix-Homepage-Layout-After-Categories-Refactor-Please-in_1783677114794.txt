Fix Homepage Layout After Categories Refactor

Please inspect the existing codebase before making any changes.

Do NOT redesign the homepage.

Do NOT create duplicate sections or duplicate components.

Root Cause

The Categories feature was recently moved from the Homepage to its own dedicated page (/categories).

However, after this refactor, the Homepage is still reserving the layout space where the old Categories section used to exist.

The content has moved to the new page, but the old Homepage wrapper/container is still being rendered, resulting in a very large blank white space between Services and Why Us.

Also inspect any imports related to Categories that are no longer being used correctly.

Required Fix

If the Categories section has intentionally been moved to /categories, then:

Completely remove the old Homepage Categories wrapper.
Remove any remaining spacing that belonged to that section.
Remove unused animation wrappers.
Remove unused layout containers.
Remove any reserved height.

Do NOT leave an empty placeholder on the Homepage.

The Homepage should flow naturally:

Hero
↓

Services
↓

Why Us
↓

Testimonials
↓

FAQ
↓

Contact

or, if Categories are intended to remain on the Homepage in the future, reconnect the existing component properly instead of leaving an empty container.

IMPORTANT

Find the root cause, not just the visual symptom.

Do not hide the issue with CSS.

Do not reduce margins just to make the gap look smaller.

Remove the actual unused component or wrapper that is occupying the space.

Verification

After fixing:

Generate a new Preview.
Verify that there is no blank white space between Services and Why Us.
Verify that the Categories page (/categories) continues to work correctly.
Verify that no functionality has been broken.
