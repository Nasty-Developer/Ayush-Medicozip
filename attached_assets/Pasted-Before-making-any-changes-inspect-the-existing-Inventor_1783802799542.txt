Before making any changes, inspect the existing Inventory Sync, Categories, Search, Medicine Cards and Product Listing logic.

Do NOT create duplicate components or duplicate sorting logic.

==================================================
STOCK PRIORITY IMPROVEMENT
==================================================

The website should prioritize medicines that are currently available.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT display the exact stock quantity to customers.

Examples:
❌ Stock: 27
❌ Quantity: 154

Instead display only:

🟢 In Stock

🟡 Limited Availability (optional)

🔴 Out of Stock

==================================================
SORTING
==================================================

Everywhere products are listed, automatically sort them in this order:

Priority 1:
In Stock

Priority 2:
Limited Availability

Priority 3:
Out of Stock

Apply this sorting to:

• Categories
• Search Results
• Browse Medicines
• New Medicines
• Special Medicines
• Related Medicines
• Admin Preview (optional)

Customers should always see purchasable products first.

==================================================
OUT OF STOCK
==================================================

Do NOT hide out of stock medicines.

Keep them visible at the bottom.

Replace Add to Cart with:

Request Medicine

Display:

"Currently unavailable"

This allows customers to still request unavailable medicines.

==================================================
INVENTORY SYNC
==================================================

When MediVision Sync runs:

• Read the actual stock from STOCK.SDF.
• Automatically update the availability status.
• Re-sort products after every sync.

If stock changes:

Out of Stock
↓

In Stock

The medicine should automatically move to the top of its category.

If stock becomes zero:

Automatically move it to the bottom.

==================================================
CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE
==================================================

Never expose internal inventory numbers.

Customers should only see availability status.

The pharmacy owner can continue seeing exact quantities inside the Admin Panel.
